== TEH Brog

Brog is a blogging rails application meant to be torn down as an engine for training purposes.

=== Requirements

* Ruby 2

* Rails 4

* Mysql

=== Setup

. cp config/database_sample.yml config/database.yml
. bundle
. rake db:create
. rake db:migrate
. rake db:seed

=== Running test

. RAILS_ENV=test rake db:migrate
. rspec
